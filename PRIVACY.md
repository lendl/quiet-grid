# Privacy Policy for Quiet Grid

Last updated: 2026-05-05

Quiet Grid is a fully offline Android puzzle app.

## What Quiet Grid collects

Quiet Grid does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

The app does not require an account and does not send data to us or to third parties.

## Data stored on your device

Quiet Grid stores a small amount of app data locally on your device so the app can work as expected. This may include:

- app settings, such as theme preference
- active puzzle state and local puzzle statistics

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.

## Ads, analytics, and third-party services

Quiet Grid does not use:

- advertising
- analytics
- tracking
- crash reporting services
- third-party online services

## Children's privacy

Quiet Grid is designed to work without collecting personal data from any user, including children.

## Changes to this policy

If Quiet Grid's privacy practices change in the future, this policy will be updated here.
