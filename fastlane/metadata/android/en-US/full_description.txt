Quiet Grid is a collection of logic puzzles for Android. No ads, no tracking, no internet connection required — everything runs entirely on your device.

**Puzzles included:**

• Takuzu — Fill a grid with 0s and 1s: each row and column must have equal counts, no three in a row, and no two rows or columns may be identical.
• Minesweeper — Classic grid puzzle. Uncover all safe cells without triggering a mine.
• Nonogram (Beta) — Deduce which cells to fill based on number clues along rows and columns.
• Sudoku (Beta) — Place digits 1–9 so every row, column, and 3×3 box contains each digit exactly once.
• Word Search (Beta) — Find hidden words in a grid of letters.

Each puzzle type offers Easy, Medium, Hard, and Expert difficulty levels.

**Privacy by design:**

Quiet Grid does not use the internet, request dangerous permissions, or collect any data. All progress and settings are stored locally on your device.

Available in English, Dutch, German, French, and Spanish.
